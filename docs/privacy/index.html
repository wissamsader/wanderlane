Privacy

Wanderlane is a simple content site. We keep data collection to a minimum.

What we collect

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand which guides are useful (aggregate page views, rough country, referring site). We don't sell data and we don't run intrusive ad networks.

Affiliate cookies

When you click an affiliate link (see our disclosure), the destination — Booking.com, GetYourGuide, and so on — may set a cookie so they can credit us if you book. Those cookies are governed by their privacy policies, not ours, and they expire on their own schedules (typically 24 hours to 30 days).

Contact

Anything you email us stays between us. To ask what we hold, or to have it deleted, write to hello@wanderlane.co.
